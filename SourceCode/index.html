SoulWorker Skill Simulator

This skill simulator require javascript turned on.
The skill simulator should work on most of browsers. I just tested on: Firefox v62, Chrome v69, Edge.

As of now, the simulator video has skill preview (and use "Promise" object). So old browsers (very old ones, Internet Explorer 11 for example) may not work well and buggy.
